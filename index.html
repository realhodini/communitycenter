M 107 · Modern Series

Tahoe

The Wellness & Social Pavilion

Architectural Description

This minimalist-style residence with very cubic shapes plays the horizontality card. The front block covered with natural wood continues in front of the main entrance and turns into an open wood lattice, accommodating the entrance and a private office.

The open-plan ground floor opens onto two terraces on either side of the house. Located in the centre of the home, the staircase is treated as a sculpture — skillfully combining the transparency of glass, the verticality of a wood lattice, and the angular black lines of the stairs.

Upstairs, a small boudoir greets you at the top of the staircase, while two bedrooms and a bathroom share the rest of the space. The roof of the garage accommodates a large terrace accessible by both bedrooms.

M 110 · Modern Series

SoHo (Custom Dark Palette)

The Industrial Event Hall

Architectural Description

The SoHo model is the result of a blend of modern styles. The architecture is composed of stacked blocks, with contrast accentuated by masonry and wood cladding. On the ground floor, service rooms are located at the front near the entrance, while the living areas are arranged around the perimeter.

A row of three garden doors opening onto the yard and occupying the entire back wall lets in light and allows for maximum enjoyment of the rear terrace. Upstairs, there are three bedrooms including a master suite with direct walk-in closet access to a private bathroom.

Custom Palette: Per Aaron's specification, this version utilizes a darker color scheme — charcoal acrylic finishes paired with deep-tinted wood — to enhance its architectural presence on the Domaine site.

N 203 · Nordic Series

Bergen

The Multi-Zone Community Pavilion

Architectural Description

The Bergen model is a Nordic-inspired design featuring a distinctive asymmetrical roofline and interlocking volumes that create a dynamic architectural silhouette. The exterior combines natural wood cladding with contemporary metal accents.

The ground floor features an open-concept living and dining area with floor-to-ceiling windows facing the rear garden. A dedicated mudroom and powder room sit near the entrance for practical everyday use.

Upstairs, three bedrooms including a master suite with ensuite bathroom provide comfortable living space, while a bonus loft area adds flexible square footage above the garage.

N 201 · Nordic Series

Oslo

The Sophisticated Event Center

Architectural Description

The Oslo model is the flagship of the Nordic Series, combining Scandinavian minimalism with generous proportions. Its signature feature is a dramatic double-height living area flanked by a sweeping staircase.

The ground floor features an expansive open-concept layout with integrated kitchen, dining, and living zones flowing seamlessly into one another. Large sliding doors open to a wraparound terrace.

The upper level houses four bedrooms including a luxurious master suite with private balcony access, walk-in closet, and spa-style ensuite bathroom.
